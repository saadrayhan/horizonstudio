

# Design Studio — Single-Page Website

A premium, editorial-style single-page website for a design-first product studio targeting early-stage founders. The site communicates calm confidence through restraint, typography contrast, and generous whitespace.

## Visual Identity
- **Typography**: Instrument Serif (headlines) + Inter (body) — the signature contrast that sets this apart
- **Colors**: Warm off-white `#FAFAF9` base, near-black `#0C0C0B` text, indigo-violet `#5B3CF5` accent, soft violet `#F0EEF8` secondary backgrounds
- **Aesthetic**: Paper-matte, flat, no gradients, minimal shadows — like a premium printed publication

## Sections (top to bottom)

1. **Sticky Navigation** — Transparent → solid on scroll with subtle border. Wordmark left, ghost links (Work, About, Contact) + filled "Start a project" CTA right

2. **Hero (full viewport)** — Left-aligned headline "Products built to think and feel right." in 88px Instrument Serif. Subline + dual CTAs below. Right side: floating UI mockup card with subtle infinite float animation

3. **Services ("What We Do")** — Three editorial rows (Product Design, Full-Stack Development, Product Strategy) with large faint background numbers (01, 02, 03), separated by thin dividers

4. **Philosophy ("How We Work")** — Four manifesto-style statements in two-column layout. Left: bold serif statement. Right: supporting explanation. Statements like "Design before code." and "We say no to projects we can't do well."

5. **Work ("Selected Work")** — Two large featured project cards with category tags, project names, outcome descriptions, and placeholder screenshot areas

6. **Footer CTA** — Violet-tinted background, large serif headline "Have something to build?", single prominent CTA

7. **Footer** — Dark background, three columns: branding, navigation links, social/copyright

## Interactions & Motion
- Sections fade up 20px on scroll with staggered timing (500ms ease-out)
- Buttons scale to 0.98 on press
- Links get left-to-right underline slide on hover
- Hero mockup has subtle floating animation (4s cycle)
- Nav border fades in on scroll

## Responsive
- All sections stack single-column on mobile
- Headlines reduce to ~60% of desktop size
- Navigation collapses to hamburger menu
